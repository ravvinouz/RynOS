🎭 Face Mask Recipes
🍯Honey + Turmeric Glow2x / Week
Target: Brightening · PIH fading · Anti-bacterial
Must Have
1 tspRaw honey
¼ tspTurmeric powder
1 tspPlain yogurt (dahi)
Optional Boost
3-4 dropsLemon juice(skip if irritated)
Method: Mix → apply thin layer → leave 15-20 min → rinse with lukewarm water → moisturize. Patch test first. Turmeric can stain — use old cloth.
Best for: cheek PIH spots. Honey antibacterial, turmeric anti-inflammatory, yogurt exfoliates gently.
🥔Potato Juice Spot TreatmentDaily
Target: PIH (Post-Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation)
Ingredients
½ potatoRaw potato, grated or sliced
cotton pador your fingertip
Method: Grate potato, squeeze out juice onto cotton pad. Apply directly to dark spots (right cheek PIH). Leave 20-30 min or overnight. Rinse. Do this every single day for visible results in 2-4 weeks.
Catecholase enzyme in potato naturally inhibits melanin. Most effective treatment you have access to right now. Do not skip.
🍋Besan + Lemon Brightening1x / Week
Target: Dullness · Dead skin · Overall brightness
Must Have
2 tspBesan (chickpea flour)
½ tspLemon juice (fresh)
1 tspRose water (or plain water)
Optional
¼ tspTurmeric
1 tspMilk (makes softer paste)
Method: Mix to smooth paste. Apply evenly. Leave 15 min (don't let it fully dry and crack). Rinse with circular scrubbing motion — acts as gentle exfoliant. Follow with SPF if going out.
Skip lemon if skin is irritated or sunburned. Besan gently exfoliates dead skin cells. Use only 1x/week max.
🥛Milk + Honey Moisture2x / Week
Target: Hydration · Soft skin · Winter dryness
Ingredients
2 tspFull-fat milk (doodh)
1 tspHoney
optional2-3 drops mustard oil or almond oil
Method: Mix together, apply with cotton pad or fingertips. Leave 15 min. Rinse gently with lukewarm water. Pat dry — don't rub. Apply moisturizer immediately after.
Lactic acid in milk gently exfoliates + brightens. Honey locks moisture. Best in winter or after harsh workouts.
🌿Aloe Vera Calm & RepairAfter sun · Post-workout
Target: Redness · Sun repair · Soothing irritated skin
Ingredients
2 tspAloe vera gel (fresh or packaged)
optional2 drops tea tree oil (spot only)
optionalRosewater — makes it more refreshing
Method: Apply aloe gel directly on face. Leave 20-30 min or overnight. Rinse or leave on as sleeping mask. No rinsing needed if overnight. Great after SPF-heavy outdoor days.
Most gentle mask. Can use daily in summer. Tea tree oil only on active pimples — never all over face.
